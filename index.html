This tool extracts transactions from your Spanish N26 joint account bank statement PDFs and converts them into YNAB-compatible CSV files. It supports multiple file uploads, making it easy to convert several statements at once.

Your CSV files will be automatically downloaded when the process is complete. This process is safe and secure—no data is uploaded to the internet; everything happens locally on your device. For more details, you can check the source code on GitHub.
